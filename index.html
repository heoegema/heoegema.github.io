Tuter Rex

An Interactive 3D Math Game

T-Rex allows kids ages 7-11 to learn basic math skills up through geometry and basic algebra. It uses a 3D-motion sensing camera to allow kids to help T-Rex on his journey through the tropical island of Pythagorean. Along the way, the kid must help T-Rex solve basic math problems as T-Rex tries to find his way back to his mother. Solving the math problems and clues along the way present a problem for T-Rex, but with help from his friend, T-Rex works his way back to find mom. Using the 3D motion camera, kids don’t type in a number, they actually show T-Rex using their hands. It is no longer an EdTech on a screen, rather kids interact with the game just like they do in school - using their hands as they learn to count and solve basic math problems.

Tuter Rex was created as a submission for Hack Harvard 2015. View Here

  • Date: November 2015
  • Category: Hackathon Project
  • Created With: Unity Game Engine and Intel Real Sense Camera

S.S. Nemesis

A Science Fiction Visual Novel

The year is 645 A.R. (After Revolution), 645 years have passed since the ‘revolution,’ a conflict that consisted of computer warfare and information control-because of this, much of history was erased, hardly any information about the past remained-all history textbooks and recordings were destroyed causing humanity to start on a clean slate, likewise, religion slowly started to dwindle-and due to lack of resources, faded over time. Christ, Muhammad and other religious figures became non-existent. However, in the shadows one religion managed to thrive; Scientology. The world’s scientologists kept hidden and formed a secret council called the ‘4,000.’ Throughout the course of hundreds of years they worked on a plan to bring about ‘Incident III,’-a method to ‘free’ humans from their bodies and achieve their true forms, using the energy created from the ‘soul release’ to free the almighty Xenu ( a legendary alien overlord: whose existence is unknown.) However, this method is not exactly guaranteed and might just result in the extinction of humanity. Meanwhile, tensions between two of the world’s superpowers; The Britannia Union (formed from the Americas, Europe and Australia), and The Sinic Republic (formed from Russia, most of the African Continent and China) have tight tensions, and rumors of a nuclear war have been floating around leaving many people in alarm. A group of politicians, businessmen and other people in power have joined to form a group called the ‘Vantaggio Circle,’ (or just ‘The Circle’ for short) in attempt to save humanity if such events were to occur. The humanity preservation project is called “S.S. Nemesis,” named after the ‘ark’ or steam powered spaceship that will be able to save the human race. However, this project is not common knowledge, seeing that the ship itself only has enough room to carry 2000 people-a mere fragment of the earth’s population. The player fills the shoes of Charlotte, a young 15 year old girl who works part time at a resort located on a small island. One day, she notices a group of very important looking people. Little does she know that these people are ‘The Circle.’ That night, the resort is attacked by a group of the Four Thousand. The Circle is slaughtered, and Charlotte encounters one of the members during his dying moments. He tells her of “The Red Door,” and to see the “Angel” and send the message that the “Group has been compromised.” With this limited knowledge, Charlotte heads to Italy in order to pass on this so called message. From here on out, your choices determine not only Charlotte’s fate, but the fate of the world as well. S.S. Nemesis is a story about the end of the world.

Tonzo

The bear that cares.

Tonzo is a mental health chatbot that uses natural language processing, and sentiment analysis to determine how a user is feeling and react in an appropriate way. Tonzo will tell you jokes when you're upset, ask you about yourself when you're happy, give you coping strategies and destressing techniques when stressed and will give you a hotline to call if you show signs of need. Tonzo also allows you to learn more about resources in your area. You can ask him to help you find a counselor, psychologist or psychiatrist and he will give you their contact information. If you ask Tonzo about various mental illnesses (depression, anxiety, ptsd etc.) He will send you a link from a reliable source (such as the Canadian Mental Health Association) so you can learn more. Tonzo was created for the facebook messenger platform.

You can view this project on github here.

  • Created: September 2016
  • Category: Chatbot
  • Platform: Facebook Messenger
  • Created with: Python, LUIS, Cognitive Services API(s), Google Places API

Run With The Right Crowd

What does what you say, say about you?

Run With The Right Crowd was a hackathon project created at QHacks 2017. The idea behind Run With The Right Crowd was that a person who was new to reddit, could input what they wanted to post and the app would tell them what the best subreddit to post it in would be. We used the Reddit Dataset from kaggle, but since the dataset was too large for us to process on our local machines, we cut it down to a fraction of its original size. I used the bag of words model in order to process reddit comments, and used the Random Forest Classifer model in order to predict what comments would be associated with what subreddit and score. We used the Indico API and pygal libraries to display other metrics such as sentiment.

You can view this project on github here.

  • Created: February 2017
  • Category: Hackathon Project
  • Platform: Web Application
  • Created with: Python, Flask, Pygal, scikit-learn

Map Now

Map Now is a freelance project I completed for a client as a MVP for their startup. It is a realtime, location based social networking site. Users are able to log on and view other other users online at the same time in their area. Users are able to enter a message, their name as well as optional links to various social media profiles, such as linkedin, facebook and twitter. Users are able to make their pin private and allow them to only be visibile to another user if they enter a unique ID and a password (also optional). Users are also able to change the location of their pin and it will update in real time across all users. The site creates cookies to remember user information and is able to detect links and email addresses and convert them appropriately. When a new user logs onto the website, the site will send an email notifying the administrator of the webiste.

  • Created: April 2017
  • Category: Freelance
  • Platform: Web Application
  • Created with: Node.JS, Materialize, Socket.io

OddJobb
